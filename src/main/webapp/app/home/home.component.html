About FF-Sports Council

The Sports Council organizes FF events, which are evenly spaced throughout the year. The main objective of the Sports committee is to develop an all-round personality of the team as well as inculcate values like teamwork, co-ordination and leadership while exploring talents. We have events for various sports like football, ludo, badminton, carom, chess etc.
